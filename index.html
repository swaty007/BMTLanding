About BMC

Unlike other cooperative mining companies, we consider investor funds safety and transparency one of our main priorities. For this reason, we provide personal cabinet for every investor with extensive information including list of active ASICs and GPUs, total hash rate, mined blocks count, electricity consumption and maintenance costs; Main Balkan mining facility is located in Serbia, which means that we have one of the lowest electricity prices in Europe for enterprises, only 6.4 â‚¬ cents per kWh, which is about 2.5 times cheaper compared to an average European household.

Project Advantages

We are a centralized mining facility, which means less costs for maintenance and facilities per unit of processing power. We have negotiated heavy discounts directly from manufacturers of specific mining equipment. We keep track of our equipment, update it exactly when it is financially beneficial.
After equipment becomes obsolete we do not throw it away but resell it, reducing capital expenditures by extra 5% to 10%.
Token holders may sell BM tokens on cryptocurrencies exchange starting from October 2018. This way investing in Balkan mining is much safer compared to regular cloud mining.

Mining Tokens
